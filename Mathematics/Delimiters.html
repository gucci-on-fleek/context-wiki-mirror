Several commands are available for different sizes of delimiters:

  • Size level 1 : \big*
  • Size level 2 : \Big*
  • Size level 3 : \bigg*
  • Size level 4 : \Bigg*

The l (left), r (right), m (middle) affects the surrounding spaces. 

  • \setuppapersize[A7]
\setupbodyfont[16pt]
\starttext
$\Biggl{\{} \biggl{\{} \Bigl{\{} \bigl{\{} {\{} {\}} \bigr{\}} \Bigr{\}} \biggr{\}} \Biggr{\}}$

\blank[small]

$\Bigg {\{} \bigg {\{} \Big {\{} \big {\{} {\{} {\}} \big {\}} \Big {\}} \bigg{\}}  \Bigg {\}}$

\blank[small]

$\Biggm{\{} \biggm{\{} \Bigm{\{} \bigm{\{} {\{} {\}} \bigm{\}} \Bigm{\}} \biggm{\}} \Biggm{\}}$
\stoptext
