Typescripts provided with ConTeXt

Few information first:

  • You can use the typescripts provided with ConTeXt , they are visible here
  • These typescripts are stored in tex/texmf-context/tex/context/fonts/mkiv/ . They are defined by the files "type-imp-*.mkiv". You can have a look with the source browser .
