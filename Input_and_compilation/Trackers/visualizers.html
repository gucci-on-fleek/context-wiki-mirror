Description

This tracker modifies the output document by adding colored bars to highlight justification. Using it, a quick scan of the document will show lines that may not be pleasingly justified.

  • Centered lines are marked by yellow bars to the left and right, in the text area.
  • Flush-right lines are marked by a cyan bar to the left of the text, in the text area.
  • Inserted line breaks are indicated by a magenta bar to the right of the text, in the text area.
  • Flush-left lines are marked by a green bar to the right of the text, in the text area
  • Justified lines are marked by a green bar in the text area, when there is no word-space compression (shrink) applied to the line.
  • Justified lines are marked by a blue bar in the right margin when compression has been applied. The width of the bar indicates the degree of compression.
  • Justified lines with severe compression may be marked with a red bar.
