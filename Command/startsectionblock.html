Summary

The instances of \startsectionblock ... \stopsectionblock delimit one of the section blocks, e.g. frontmatter and backmatter.

Settings

\startsectionblock[...][...=...,...] ... \stopsectionblock
[...] name
bookmark text
...=...,... inherits from \setupsectionblock
