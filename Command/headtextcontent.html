Summary

The command \headtextcontent is used for getting the headline content.

Description

Examples

Example 1 

  • \defineframed
  [ChapterFrame]
  [width=\textwidth,
   align=flushright,
   offset=0pt,
   rulethickness=0pt]

\startsetups [christian_dietrich]
  \startlocalheadsetup
    \startframed[ChapterFrame]
      \blank[2cm]
      \startfont[Serif sa 8]
        \color[darkgray]{\headnumbercontent}
      \stopfont
      \blank[5mm]
      \startfont[SerifBold sa 2]
        \color[darkred]{\headtextcontent}
      \stopfont
      \blank[1cm]
      \startfont[SerifBold sa 1.5]
        \color[darkred]{\structureuservariable{subtitle}}
      \stopfont
    \stopframed
  \stoplocalheadsetup
\stopsetups

\defineheadalternative
  [christian_dietrich]
  [renderingsetup=christian_dietrich]

\setuphead
  [chapter]
  [alternative=christian_dietrich,
   header=empty,
   style=]

\starttext

\startchapter[title=Chapter title][subtitle=Chapter subtitle]
\samplefile{lorem}
\stopchapter

\stoptext
