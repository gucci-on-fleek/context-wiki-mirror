Summary

The instances of \mathframed are used for putting frames, margins and backgrounds around a block of math commands.

Instances

\mframed
\inmframed baseline-aligned version of \mframed
\mcframed

Settings instance

\MATHFRAMED[...=...,...]{...}
...=...,... inherits from \setupmathframed
{...} content
