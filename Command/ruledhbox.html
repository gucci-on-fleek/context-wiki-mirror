Syntax

\ruledhbox{...}
{...} text

Description

Draw the argument's hbox as a grey line, with a dotted line where the baseline is. The dotted line may be on the boundary of the hbox.

Example 

  • \switchtobodyfont[20pt]
H

H \ruledhbox{\lohi{\textbackslash}{/}} 
\lohi
  {\ruledhbox{\textbackslash}}
  {\ruledhbox{/}},

H

H

H \ruledhbox{\lohi{o}{x}} 
\lohi
  {\ruledhbox{o}}
  {\ruledhbox{x}},
