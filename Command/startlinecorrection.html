Summary

The environment \startlinecorrection ... \stoplinecorrection grid-aligns a block

Settings

\startlinecorrection[...,...] ... \stoplinecorrection
[...,...] blank inherits from \blank

Description

Meant for stuff like formulae that needs a different lineheight to snap back to the line grid.

Also usable for frame based stuff like images and natural tables.

Not meant for section titles.
