Summary

The command \placeontopofeachother stacks two items

Settings

\placeontopofeachother{...}{...}
{...} content
{...} content

Description

Stacks two items on top of each other, centered horizontally. Creates a separate paragraph unless wrapped in a \vbox

Examples

Example 1 

  • A \placeontopofeachother{Big}{Wonderful} World.

\blank[2*line]


A \vbox{\placeontopofeachother{Big}{Wonderful}} World.
