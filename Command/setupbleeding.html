Summary

The command \setupbleeding is used to define stretching of graphics to extend them over the edge of the paper into the bleed (cutspace).

Settings

\setupbleeding[...=...,...]
width dimension
height dimension
offset dimension
hoffset dimension
voffset dimension
location t b l r bl lb br rb tl lt tr rt
stretch yes no
page yes no
