How to edit descriptions

To edit the descripiton of a command, click on 'edit' next to the word 'Description'. You can use all the formatting of the mediawiki wiki markup, except that the piped table syntax is not implemented yet. The following interwiki links are available: 

[[cmd:goto]] 
[[wiki:layout]]
[[manual:somemanual.pdf]]

This list will be expanded in the near future.

You can now also typeset tex and metapost sourcecode with <code source="tex">...</code> and source="mp".
