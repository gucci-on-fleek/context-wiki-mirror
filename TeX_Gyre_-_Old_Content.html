Termes (aka Times New Roman)

For MKII: 

  • \usetypescript[times][ec] % leave out 'ec' when using XeTeX
\setupbodyfont[times,9pt,rm]

Hello Termes! 1234567890


For MKIV: 

\usetypescript[termes]
\setupbodyfont[termes,12pt]
